Trading Desk (Position Tracker) Mockup Analysis — from index.html
================================================================

OVERVIEW:
- The Position Tracker is now called "Trading Desk" going forward
- It's a 150-day compounding challenge tracker with live trade management
- Terminal Noir theme with JetBrains Mono + Inter fonts

STRUCTURE:
1. TABS: "My Trades LIVE" (active, cyan border) | "AI Trades PAPER" (yellow badge)
2. SUMMARY BAR: Current Day (7 of 150), Capital (₹1,34,128), Profit (+₹34,128 / 34.1%), 
   Today's P&L (+₹950 with Exit All ×), Target Remaining (₹5,750), Schedule (+₹6,500 / +1d)
3. TABLE: 15 columns — Day, Date, Open Capital, Target (5%), Proj Capital, Instrument, Type, 
   Strike, Entry, LTP, Qty, P&L, Actual Capital, Deviation, Rating

ROW TYPES:
- Past days (row-past): Green tint, completed trades, collapsed (no Type/Strike/Entry/LTP details)
- Gift days (row-gift): Gold tint, auto-completed from excess profit, all dashes for trade details
- Multi-day (row-past with star): Same day index spans multiple calendar dates
- Today (row-today): Yellow highlight, left yellow border, full trade details visible
- Today sub-rows (row-today-sub): Individual trades within today, dimmed day/capital columns
- Future (row-future): Dimmed (45% opacity), projected values, dashes for trade columns
- New trade input row (row-new-trade): Green left border, slide-in animation

TODAY'S TRADE STATES:
- Closed TP: Green "✓ TP" badge, no exit button
- Closed SL: Red "✗ SL" badge, no exit button  
- Closed Partial: Green "✓ Partial" badge, no exit button
- OPEN in profit: Yellow pulsing dot "OPEN" badge, green (×) exit button next to P&L
- OPEN in loss: Yellow pulsing dot "OPEN" badge, red (×) exit button next to P&L
- Short OPEN in profit: Same OPEN badge, green (×) exit

NEW TRADE INPUT ROW:
- "NEW" label in date column
- Instrument dropdown (NIFTY 50, BANK NIFTY, CRUDE OIL, NAT GAS)
- Side toggle: B (green) | S (red)
- Type toggle: CE (green) | PE (red)
- Strike input field
- Entry input field
- LTP auto-fill (italic, secondary color)
- Qty input with ~% of capital hint below
- Confirm (✓) and Cancel (×) buttons in P&L column

TODAY SUMMARY ROW:
- "DAY 7 TOTAL" label
- (+) New trade button in LTP column
- Total Qty
- Total P&L with Exit All (×) button
- Actual Capital
- Deviation

INSTRUMENT TAGS (color-coded):
- NIFTY 50: Blue (#60a5fa)
- BANK NIFTY: Purple (#a78bfa)
- CRUDE OIL: Yellow/Gold (#fbbf24)
- NAT GAS: Green (#34d399)

TYPE COLUMN:
- B CE, B PE = Green (Buy/Long)
- S CE, S PE = Red (Sell/Short)

RATING SYSTEM:
- 🏆 = Target hit (5%)
- 🏆🏆 = Big win (≥10%)
- 🎁 = Gift day (auto-completed from excess)
- ⭐ = Multi-day (spans multiple calendar dates)
- ⬜ = Future (not yet reached)
- 🏁 = Final day (Day 150)

DEVIATION COLUMN:
- Shows +/- amount vs projected capital
- Shows days ahead/behind in parentheses: (0d), (+2d), etc.

CAPITAL FLOW:
- Day 1 starts at ₹1,00,000
- Target = 5% of Open Capital
- Proj Capital = Open Capital + Target
- Actual Capital = Open Capital + realized P&L
- Day 150 projects to ₹1,57,23,309

KEY DESIGN DETAILS:
- Sticky table header
- Max height 600px with scrollbar
- Hover highlights entire row
- Past days show collapsed trade details (dashes for Type/Strike/Entry/LTP)
- Today shows full trade details per trade
- Future days show projected capital calculations only
- Scrollbar styled to match theme
